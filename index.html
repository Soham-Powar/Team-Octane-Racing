About COEP Tech

COEP Technological University, formerly known as the College of Engineering Pune (COEP), is one of India's oldest and most prestigious engineering institutions. Established in 1854, COEP Tech is renowned for its strong emphasis on practical and hands-on learning. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various engineering disciplines. COEP Tech is also known for its vibrant student clubs, innovative projects, and collaborations with industry leaders, making it a hub for aspiring engineers and technologists.

INTRODUCTION

At Team Octane Racing Electric, we don't just build cars—we engineer speed, precision, and innovation. Powered by passion and inspired by Formula One, our team from COEP Technological University builds high-performance electric race cars, equipped with sophisticated high-voltage systems and lightweight carbon fiber components. And this year, we are representing India at two International events - Formula Student Germany and Formula Student East. Be a part of our journey to drive the future of racing!

WHY HELP US?

Your support helps us in:

  • Vehicle Logistic: Shipping our car from India to Europe.
  • Manufacturing: Building high-performance components with precision and quality.
  • Competition Registration: To register for the Formula Student Germany and Formula Student East.
  • Driver Safety and Gears: To buy safety equipments for our drivers.

Together, let's redefine student motorsports!

PROGRESS TILL NOW...

Thanks to generous corporate sponsorships and college funding, we’ve successfully covered most of our vehicle’s manufacturing costs. Now, we need your support to fund critical components and transport our car overseas for Formula Student East & Germany 2025! You can be a part of this journey by contributing to our campaign—every bit of support brings us closer to representing India on the global stage. Join us and help drive our dream forward!

A TEAM ABOVE ALL, ABOVE ALL A TEAM

We at Octane Racing, are a team of 30+ ambitious engineers from various disciplines of COEP Tech. We design, manufacture, and race an F1-styled, single-seater, electric vehicle to participate in various national and international competitions conducted by SAE.

This year, we're setting our sights on the top step of the international podium and building our most advanced electric race car yet. We're pushing the boundaries in every aspect, from an all-new 600V battery pack, highly powerful and efficient hub motor assembly, carbon fiber structural parts, as well as an aerodynamic kit. But to translate these dreams into reality, we need your support!

WHY CROWDFUNDING?

Formula Student is an expensive endeavor. The cutting-edge materials and components needed to build a competitive race car come at a premium. While COEP Tech provides invaluable support, crowdfunding allows us to bridge the gap and make our vision a reality.

YOUR CONTRIBUTION MAKES A DIFFERENCE!

Every contribution, big or small, gets us closer to the starting line. Your support will directly fund:

  • High-performance components: Lightweight materials for the structural components, powerful electric motors, and high-capacity battery pack
  • Advanced manufacturing: Access to specialized tools and techniques for creating a top-notch race car.
  • Rigorous testing: Maximum testing for data acquisition, squeezing out maximum performance from the car while ensuring complete safety

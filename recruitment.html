Recruitment Process

ROADSHOW

1st WEEK OF OCTOBER

Experience the thrill of speed with Team Octane Racing Electric at our roadshow! Watch our high-performance Formula Student car in action, showcasing cutting-edge design and engineering. Get ready for an adrenaline-packed event that brings motorsport innovation to life!

PRIMARY INTERVIEW

2nd WEEK OF OCTOBER

In this round, we’re turning up the heat with questions that will put your high school knowledge and problem-solving skills to the test. Physics, math, and sharp aptitude challenges will come your way as we look for those who can think fast and deliver under pressure. This is your chance to show us the genius within and prove why you’re the next game-changer for our electric racing team!

TASK ROUND

3rd WEEK OF OCTOBER

This is where the real fun begins—time to break down the task you tackled and show us how you made magic happen. We’ll dig into your process, teamwork style, and how you conquered those tricky obstacles. We're not just looking for skills, we're looking for next-level innovators who can think on their feet and own the challenge. This is your moment to shine as the ultimate problem-solver and claim your spot in the pit crew of legends!
